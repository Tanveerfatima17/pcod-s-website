  • Why periods are irregular?
    • The term irregular period consists of both early and delay of menstrual cycle.Also their is difference between irregularities of menstrual cycle of young women and women.
    In young women whose period is just started or started between one to three years ago the irregularity is either too close (duration of approx. 21 days between two periods) or too far(duration of approx. 45 days between two periods).Whereas in case of adult women whose period had been started more than three years, the irregularity is either to close (duration of approx. 21 days between two periods) or too far(duration of approx. 35 days between two periods).
    The reasons for "why periods are irregular" could be many. Few reaons are mentioned below, though these reasons may seem common or not harmful but they may affect menstrual cycle.

    1]Sleep Cycle:
    Having an unhealthy sleep cycle and poor sleep quality leads to increase in cortisol hormone (which plays an important role in stress response) and this leads to decrease of leptin hormone (which is released for balancing energy level in body), such condition leads to the delay of ovulation period and hence irregularity in menstrual cycle occurs.

    2]Stress:
    As mentioned in sleep cycle point cortisol hormone is at its peak level when we are stressed whereas its level should be more in early morning and less in night or midnight. So this hormone disturbs the balance of progesterone-estrogen hormones which leads to irregular periods.

    3]Medicines:
    In many young women, the continue or over-use of birth control pills also highly affects irregularity and disturbs the hormonal harmony of body.

    4]Weight changes:
    Suddenly gaining or loosing weight also following specific strict diet or eating unhealthy food causes irregularity.

  • PCOS and Weight Relation
    • Managing weight while suffering with PCOS is very difficult for many women and girls. During this weight fluctuates with two types that is gaining of weight and loosing of weight. Hence being healthy and possessing healthy weight is main goal while suffering with PCOS. Weight is generally measured using Body Mass Index(BMI). It is calculated by dividing your weight in kilograms by your height in metered squared.
    According to WHO (World Health Organization) BMI ranges are as follows:
    BMI of less than 18.5 = under weight
    BMI between 18.5-24.9 = healthy weight
    BMI between 25.0-29.9 = unhealthy weight
    BMI greater than 30 = very unhealthy weight
    Possessing overweight may cause some risky health problems like heart disease, stroke, cancer and some psychological challenges. Keeping record of change in weight with waist measurement is also helpful. In general having large waist is mostly seen in case of women and women whose waist measurement is 80cm or more are at high risk of health problems like diabetes and heart diseases.
